Does your TATech
prioritize an outstanding Candidate Experience?

Whether a candidate approaches you or you identify them, securing their consent and offering response choices is crucial for a great candidate experience. Using the recruiter's authentic voice fosters trust, strengthens employer branding, and makes candidates more eager to engage and interview.

Does your TATech ensure EEOC and other compliances?

EEOC guidelines mandate AI technology to uphold Transparency with explainable AI algorithms; Bias Mitigation against factors like race, color, religion, and gender; Human Oversight to safeguard protected groups; Fairness & Consistency, ensuring equal treatment for all job applicants.

Does your TATech integrate seamlessly with your systems of records?

Many systems of records like ATS, HRIS while do a great job of record keeping; many lack proper automation around candidate interaction, CV matching, scheduling, interviewing, and matching to job opportunities. Today a good TATech has strong integrations with leading systems of records including SuccessFactors, Workday, iCIMS, ORC etc.

What problems we solve for TA Organizations?

Incomplete Job Description (JD)

We ensure your job description is comprehensive and unbiased. We analyze and refine JDs, addressing any gaps, and can even craft one from scratch if you lack one!

Difficulty in
JD – CV Matching

Many ATSs struggle with JD-CV matching, leading to manual screening. We'll match and score CVs against your JD, presenting them with a fitment score and a slider for targeted selection.

Screening Interview Questions

Crafting precise interview questions for every skill is challenging. We auto-generate accurate questions and answers to assess Skills, Experience, and Competence.

Candidate Personalization

We facilitate interactions in the recruiter's own voice (record and review on our platform), leading to improved candidate responses, candid answers, and heightened job interest.

Multi-modal interactions

Candidate have a choice whether they want to respond via text & voice or phone & voice. We leverage multi-modal outreach via text, emails, bots and WhatsApp etc.

End-to-end automation

We go beyond screening: assisting with interview scheduling, conducting interviews, announcing offers, prompting acceptances, and engaging until the candidate's onboard.
